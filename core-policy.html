Privacy Policy

Core — gym & club management app  ·  Package: com.mar.gym

Effective date: 9 July 2026  ·  Last updated: 9 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Core ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use our mobile application. Core is a gym and sports-club management app used by a club's owner and their employees to manage members and subscriptions, record attendance (including by scanning a QR code), manage staff and payroll, track products and equipment, and view income, expenses, and reports.

By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Who We Are

Data controller: Core
Application: Core (com.mar.gym)
Contact: support@core-gym.app

Please update the app name, contact address, and (if applicable) your company name above to your official details before publishing.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information — when the owner creates your account or you sign in, we collect your full name, email address, phone number, a password (stored by our backend in encrypted/hashed form), and your role (owner/admin or staff).
  • Member & business data you enter — information you record about the club to run it: members/players (name, age, phone, subscription type and dates, optional photo), staff records, memberships and renewals, attendance check-ins, payroll, products and equipment, and income/expense entries.
  • Images — optional member or profile photos you choose to add from your device camera or photo library.
  • App preferences — settings such as theme (light/dark), language (English/Arabic), and accent color. These are stored on your device.
  • Support communications — information you send us when you contact support.
About member data: Core is a business tool. Data you enter about your members, staff, and finances is provided by you, the account holder, and you are responsible for having the right to enter it and for informing your members as required by applicable law. We process it on your behalf to provide the service.

2.2 Camera

  • QR attendance scanning — with your permission, the App uses the camera to scan a member's QR code to record attendance. The camera feed is used only to read the code; we do not record or store video.
  • Photos — with your action, the camera or photo library may be used to attach a member or profile picture.

2.3 Information collected automatically

  • Push notification token — a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to deliver alerts and notifications (for example, subscription-expiry reminders) to your device.
  • Device & diagnostic information — basic technical details (such as device model, operating system version, and language) and logs needed to operate and troubleshoot the App.
  • Authentication tokens — access and refresh tokens that keep you signed in, stored in the device's secure storage.
We do not:
  • show advertising or use an Advertising ID (the App does not use the Android Advertising ID).
  • sell your personal information.
  • collect your device's location.
  • access your microphone.
  • access the camera or photo library without your action and permission.

4. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to run the service:

  • Backend (Supabase) — data you submit is sent to and stored on our backend, hosted on Supabase, to provide authentication, database, and file storage for the service.
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — used to deliver push notifications. Google processes the notification token and delivery data under Google's privacy terms.
  • Google Sign-In — if you choose to sign in with Google, Google provides us your basic profile (name, email) to create or match your account.
  • Within your club — data is visible to the owner and the employees the owner grants access to, according to their role (staff cannot see salaries, payroll, or financial reports).
  • Legal and safety — we may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the service.

We do not sell your personal data and do not share it with advertisers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase (backend, auth, database, storage) — supabase.com/privacy
  • Google Firebase (Cloud Messaging) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Sign-In — policies.google.com/privacy

Opening external links, phone (tel:), or email (mailto:) actions from within Core hands you off to those apps, which have their own policies.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • Sensitive credentials and authentication tokens are stored using the device's secure storage (Android Keystore / encrypted storage).
  • Access to club data is restricted by role on our backend, so employees cannot access owner-only information such as salaries, payroll, and financial reports.
  • We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We keep your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

8. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & correction — you can view and edit your profile information in the App, and the owner can manage accounts.
  • Permissions — you can grant or revoke Camera and Notification permissions at any time in your device settings; the App still works for tasks that don't need them.
  • Account deletion — you can request deletion of your account and associated personal data by following our Delete Your Account instructions or by contacting us at support@core-gym.app.
  • Notifications — you can turn off push notifications in your device settings or in the App.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable laws (such as GDPR), including the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your data, and to data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details above.

9. Children's Privacy

Core is a business tool intended for gym owners and their staff and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children for their own use. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located in countries other than your own. Where required, we take steps to ensure your data is treated securely and in accordance with this policy.
