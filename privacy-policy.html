Data Controller

Bawabat Al Hajj is operated by the app developer. For any privacy-related requests, contact us using the details below.

Introduction

Welcome to Bawabat Al Hajj ("the App", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy rights. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Who We Are

Bawabat Al Hajj is a digital pilgrimage support application designed to assist Hajj pilgrims in managing their journey, group data, training, and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (securely hashed)
  • National ID / Saudi ID (if applicable)
  • Pilgrim and travel group data

2.2 Automatically Collected

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App version
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging token (for notifications only)
  • Location data (only with your permission)

2.3 We Do NOT Collect

  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No behavioral analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase Analytics)
  • No biometric data
  • No payment or financial data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Displaying pilgrimage-related information
  • Prayer time calculation
  • Qibla direction
  • Push notifications
  • Complaint handling and support

4. Data Storage and Security

On Device

  • Secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain)
  • Local caching using SharedPreferences

On Servers

  • Encrypted HTTPS communication
  • Hashed passwords (never stored in plain text)
  • Access-controlled databases

5. Data Sharing

  • With your Hajj group or travel office (for operational purposes only)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) for push notifications only
  • Legal authorities if required by law

We do NOT sell or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Control app permissions anytime via device settings

7. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with applicable data protection laws (including GDPR where applicable).

10. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (notifications only)
  • YouTube Player (video playback)
  • Currency API (fxapi.app)

11. Cookies

We do not use cookies. We only use local storage for app functionality.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App means acceptance of the updated version.
