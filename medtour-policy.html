Privacy Policy

MedTour mobile application  ·  Package: com.mar.medtour

Effective date: 8 July 2026  ·  Last updated: 8 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MedTour ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. MedTour is a medical-tourism platform that helps users discover and connect with medical centers, doctors, hospitals, hotels, car agencies, and related services.

By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Who We Are

Data controller: MedTour
Application: MedTour (com.mar.medtour)
Service backend: medtour-sy.com
Contact: support@medtour-sy.com

Please update the contact address above to your official support email before publishing if it differs.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information — when you register or sign in, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and a password (stored in encrypted/hashed form).
  • Profile information — profile photo and any details you add to your profile.
  • Business / service listing data — if you manage a listing (medical center, hotel, car agency, doctor, etc.), the addresses, descriptions, photos, and location you provide.
  • User content — reviews, ratings, favorites, orders/bookings, messages, and documents or images you choose to upload.
  • Support communications — information you send us when you contact support.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Location data — with your permission, we access your approximate and/or precise device location to show nearby services, set a business address, and display map results. Location is used while you use the relevant features and is not tracked in the background.
  • Device information — basic device details (such as device model, operating system version, and language) to operate and troubleshoot the App.
  • Push notification token — a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to deliver notifications to your device.
  • Usage / diagnostic data — technical logs needed to keep the App working reliably.

2.3 Camera, photos, and microphone

  • Camera — accessed only when you choose to take a photo or record a video (e.g., for a profile photo or attachment).
  • Photos / gallery — accessed only when you choose to select an existing image or video to upload. On Android 13+ the system Photo Picker is used, so the App only receives the specific items you select.
  • Microphone — accessed only if you record a video that includes audio.
We do not:
  • show advertising or use an Advertising ID (the App explicitly opts out of the Android Advertising ID).
  • sell your personal information.
  • access your location, camera, microphone, or media without your action and permission.

4. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to run the service:

  • MedTour backend — data you submit is sent to and stored on our servers at medtour-sy.com over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection.
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — used to deliver push notifications. Google processes the notification token and delivery data under Google's privacy terms.
  • Other users — content you choose to make public (such as listings, reviews, and ratings) is visible to other users of the App.
  • Legal and safety — we may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the service.

We do not sell your personal data and do not share it with advertisers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Cloud Messaging) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Map tiles / geolocation — used to display maps and nearby results.

Opening external links, phone (tel:), email (mailto:), or map apps from within MedTour hands you off to those apps, which have their own policies.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • All network communication with our servers uses encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • Sensitive credentials and authentication tokens are stored using the device's secure storage (Android Keystore / encrypted storage).
  • We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We keep your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

8. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & correction — you can view and edit your profile information in the App.
  • Permissions — you can grant or revoke Location, Camera, Microphone, Photos, and Notification permissions at any time in your device settings.
  • Account deletion — you can request deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting us at support@medtour-sy.com.
  • Notifications — you can turn off push notifications in your device settings.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable laws (such as GDPR), including the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your data, and to data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details above.

9. Children's Privacy

MedTour is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located in countries other than your own. Where required, we take steps to ensure your data is treated securely and in accordance with this policy.

11. Changes to This Policy
