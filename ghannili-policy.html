Privacy Policy

Ghannili (Syria Music) — music player app  ·  Package: com.mar.ghannili

Effective date: 16 July 2026  ·  Last updated: 16 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ghannili ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. Ghannili is a music player app that plays audio files stored on your device, lets you organize them into playlists, and offers a curated collection of Syrian revolution songs that you can stream or download for offline listening.

By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Who We Are

Data controller: Ghannili
Application: Ghannili / Syria Music (com.mar.ghannili)
Contact: support@ghannili.app

2. Information We Collect

The short version: Ghannili does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, or phone number, and does not collect your location. Your music library, playlists, downloads, and preferences stay on your device.

2.1 Data stored only on your device

  • Your music library — with your permission, the App reads the audio files stored on your device so it can list and play them. Your audio files are never uploaded anywhere.
  • Playlists & playback state — playlists you create, favorites, and your recent playback position are saved locally in the App's private storage.
  • Downloaded songs — songs from the revolution songs collection that you download for offline listening are saved in the App's private storage on your device.
  • App preferences — settings such as theme and language are stored on your device.

2.2 Information processed to provide the online collection

  • Network requests — when you browse, stream, or download songs from the online collection, the App contacts our backend (hosted on Supabase). Like any internet service, the server technically receives your IP address and basic request data to deliver the content. We do not use this to identify or profile you.
  • Basic device information — the App checks your device's Android version locally to request the correct media permission. This information is not transmitted to us.
We do not:
  • require an account, sign-in, or registration.
  • collect your name, email address, or phone number.
  • show advertising or use an Advertising ID (the App does not use the Android Advertising ID).
  • sell or share your personal information.
  • collect your device's location.
  • access your camera, microphone, or contacts.
  • upload your personal audio files or playlists anywhere.

3. How We Use Information

Purpose Data used
List and play the music stored on your device Audio files on your device (with your permission)
Remember your playlists, favorites, and playback position Local app data on your device
Stream and download songs from the online collection Network requests to our backend (IP address, request data)
Keep music playing in the background A media playback notification and foreground service on your device
Request the correct permission for your Android version Device OS version (processed locally only)

4. Permissions the App Uses

Permission Why the App needs it
Music & audio (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO / storage) To find and play the audio files stored on your device. If you deny it, you can still use the online songs collection.
Internet To browse, stream, and download songs from the online collection.
Foreground service & wake lock (media playback) To keep playing music with the screen off or while you use other apps, with a standard media notification.

5. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party service, governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase (backend, database, file storage) — hosts the online songs collection that the App streams and downloads from — supabase.com/privacy

6. Data Storage & Security

  • Playlists, downloads, and preferences are stored in the App's private storage, which other apps cannot access on modern Android versions.
  • Communication with our backend happens over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.
  • We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the service. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

Because your data lives on your device, you are in control of it. Data stays on your device until you delete it: you can remove downloaded songs and playlists in the App, clear the App's storage in your device settings, or uninstall the App to remove everything. Standard, short-lived server logs on our backend are kept only as long as needed to operate and secure the service.

8. Your Rights & Choices

  • Permissions — you can grant or revoke the music & audio permission at any time in your device settings; the App still works for the online collection without it.
  • Deleting your data — see our Delete Your Data page for how to remove everything the App stores.
  • Notifications — the media playback notification appears only while music is playing and can be dismissed by stopping playback.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable laws (such as GDPR). Because we do not hold personal data about you on our servers, most of these rights are exercised directly on your device. For any questions or requests, contact us using the details above.

9. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. It requires no account and stores its data locally on the device. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. International Data Transfers

The online songs collection may be served from servers located in countries other than your own. Where required, we take steps to ensure requests are handled securely and in accordance with this policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
