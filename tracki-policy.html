Privacy Policy

Tracki mobile application  ·  Package: com.muneerradwan.tracki.tracki

Effective date: 8 July 2026  ·  Last updated: 8 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tracki ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. Tracki is a GPS fleet and vehicle-tracking app that lets you view your tracking devices and vehicles on a live map, define geofences, review location history, and receive movement and geofence alerts.

By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Who We Are

Data controller: Tracki
Application: Tracki (com.muneerradwan.tracki.tracki)
Contact: support@tracki.app

Please update the contact address and, if applicable, your company name and service website above to your official details before publishing.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information — when you sign in, we collect your username, full name, email address, phone number, and a password (stored by our backend in encrypted/hashed form).
  • Profile information — a profile photo and any profile details associated with your account.
  • Tracking configuration — the tracking devices and vehicles linked to your account, the geofences (named map areas) you create, and commands you send to your devices.
  • Team members — if you invite other people to your account, the email addresses and roles you assign to them.
  • App preferences — settings such as theme, map type, distance unit, auto-refresh interval, and notification preferences. These are stored on your device.
  • Support communications — information you send us when you contact support.

2.2 Location data

  • Your device location (foreground) — with your permission, we access your device's precise and/or approximate location to center the map on you, help you place geofence centers, and show your position relative to your tracked vehicles.
  • Background location — the App requests background location access so it can keep monitoring geofences and location-based alerts for your vehicles while the App is not in the foreground. You are asked to grant this separately, and you can decline or revoke it at any time in your device settings; core viewing features continue to work without it.
  • Tracked device / vehicle location — the App displays the GPS positions, routes, and location history reported by the tracking devices registered to your account. This vehicle/asset location data is provided to and served from our backend, not collected from your phone's sensors.

2.3 Information collected automatically

  • Push notification token — a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to deliver alerts and notifications to your device.
  • Device & diagnostic information — basic technical details (such as device model, operating system version, and language) and logs needed to operate and troubleshoot the App.
  • Authentication tokens — access and refresh tokens that keep you signed in, stored in the device's secure storage.
We do not:
  • show advertising or use an Advertising ID (the App explicitly opts out of the Android Advertising ID).
  • sell your personal information.
  • access your location without your action and permission.
  • access your camera, microphone, or photo gallery.

4. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to run the service:

  • Tracki backend — data you submit is sent to and stored on our servers to provide the tracking service.
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — used to deliver push notifications. Google processes the notification token and delivery data under Google's privacy terms.
  • Map tiles — map imagery is loaded from a map-tile provider to render the map.
  • Other members of your account — tracking data, devices, and geofences are visible to the members you invite to your account.
  • Legal and safety — we may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the service.

We do not sell your personal data and do not share it with advertisers.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • Sensitive credentials and authentication tokens are stored using the device's secure storage (Android Keystore / encrypted storage).
  • We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We keep your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

8. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & correction — you can view and edit your profile information in the App.
  • Permissions — you can grant or revoke Location (including background), and Notification permissions at any time in your device settings.
  • Account deletion — you can request deletion of your account and associated personal data by following our Delete Your Account instructions or by contacting us at support@tracki.app.
  • Notifications — you can turn off push notifications in your device settings or in the App.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable laws (such as GDPR), including the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your data, and to data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details above.

9. Children's Privacy
