Delete Your Account & Data

Core  ·  Package: com.mar.gym

This page explains how to request deletion of your Core account and the personal data associated with it. It applies to the Core gym & club management app.

How to request account deletion

To request that your account and associated data be deleted, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@core-gym.app from the email address linked to your Core account.
  2. Use the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  3. Include the full name and the phone number registered on your account so we can verify your identity.
  4. We will verify your request and delete your account. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Request account deletion by email

Owner & staff accounts: Employee accounts are created and managed by the gym owner. A staff member can also ask their gym owner to remove their access from within the app. Deleting your personal login does not, by itself, erase the club's business records (see below).
Timeline: We process verified deletion requests within 30 days. Once your request is verified, your account is deactivated immediately and your personal data is permanently deleted within this period.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove the following personal data:

Deleted Description
Account information Full name, email address, phone number, password, and role.
Profile data Profile photo and any details tied to your profile.
Device identifiers Your push-notification (FCM) token and associated device records.

Business & member records

Core is a business tool. Data about the club — its members/players, staff, memberships, attendance, inventory, and finances — belongs to the club account, not to an individual employee login. Removing a staff member's personal login does not delete the club's business records. If you are the club owner and want the entire club account and all its data deleted, say so in your request and we will remove it.

What data may be kept

We may retain limited information after deletion only where required:

  • Legal & financial records — transaction or invoice records that we are legally required to keep may be retained for the period required by applicable law, then deleted.
  • Backups — residual copies in encrypted backups are purged on our normal backup rotation (no longer than 90 days).
  • Anonymized data — aggregated data that can no longer identify you may be retained for analytics and service improvement.

Questions

If you have any questions about deleting your account or data, contact us at support@core-gym.app. For full details on how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.
