Data Controller

Bawabat Al Hajj is operated by an individual developer acting as the Data Controller for personal data processing. Introduction

Welcome to Bawabat Al Hajj ("the App", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy rights. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application on Android and iOS devices.

1. Who We Are

Bawabat Al Hajj is a digital pilgrimage support application designed to assist Hajj pilgrims in managing their journey, group data, training, and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (securely hashed — never stored in plain text)
  • National ID / Saudi ID (where applicable — treated as sensitive data)
  • Gender
  • Pilgrim barcode / Hajj ID number
  • Pilgrim and travel group data
  • Images or photos you voluntarily attach when submitting complaints or support requests
  • Text content you submit in complaints, evaluations, or survey responses

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App version
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token — used for push notifications only
  • Training progress, complaint status, and certificate completion status — stored as part of your account
  • Location (precise and approximate) — used only in real-time for prayer time calculation and Qibla direction. Location is processed on-device and is never stored on our servers and never transmitted or shared. Only approximate location is cached locally on-device for the current session and is not linked to your identity.
  • Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) — aggregated, pseudonymous in-app usage metrics tied to a randomly generated Firebase Instance ID (not your name, email, or pilgrim ID). Specifically: screens viewed (e.g., Home, Prayers, Qibla), in-app events (e.g., login, complaint submitted, evaluation completed, certificate viewed), session duration, app version, device model and OS version, country (derived from IP — the IP itself is not stored), and language setting. Firebase Analytics does not collect the contents of complaints or messages, your name, email, password, National or Saudi ID, or precise GPS coordinates.

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No advertising IDs or ad tracking
  • No biometric data
  • No payment or financial information
  • No contacts, call logs, or SMS data
  • No audio recordings (audio playback for Quran is local only — no recording or upload)

4. Data Storage, Retention, and Security

Retention Periods

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Upon deletion request, data is removed within 30 days unless legal retention is required.
  • Location data: Never stored on servers — used only in real-time on-device.
  • Complaint images and text: Retained while the complaint is open; deleted after resolution or within 90 days.
  • Training and activity data: Retained while your account is active; deleted with account deletion.
  • FCM tokens: Refreshed automatically by Firebase; not stored beyond active session needs.
  • Firebase Analytics data: Retained by Google for up to 14 months in aggregated form; not linked to your account identity.

On-Device Storage

  • Credentials stored in Android Keystore / iOS Keychain
  • App preferences cached locally using SharedPreferences / UserDefaults (no sensitive personal data)
  • Certificates and images may be saved to your device's photo library with your permission (Android: WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / iOS: NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription)

Server-Side Security

  • All communications use encrypted HTTPS (TLS)
  • Passwords are hashed before storage — never stored in plain text
  • Databases are access-controlled and restricted to authorized personnel

5. Data Sharing

  • Your Hajj group / travel office: Name, phone number, barcode, and group data shared as needed to manage your pilgrimage group.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google LLC): Your FCM device token is shared with Firebase solely to deliver push notifications. Firebase does not use this for advertising or profiling.
  • Firebase Analytics (Google LLC): Aggregated, pseudonymous usage events are sent to Google as our service provider under the Firebase Data Processing Terms. The data is not used for advertising.
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose data if required by applicable law or court order.
We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties for commercial purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and associated data
  • Restriction: Request that we limit processing of your data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format (GDPR users)
  • Withdraw Consent: You may revoke permissions at any time via your device settings (location, camera, notifications, photo library). Analytics collection can also be disabled by contacting us at the email below.
  • Opt-out of sale (CCPA): We do not sell personal data. No opt-out mechanism is needed.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at developer@syrianhajj.org.

8. Account and Data Deletion

You may request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time using any of the following methods:

In-App (Recommended)

  1. Open the app and go to the More screen (bottom navigation)
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Remove Account"
  3. Confirm your decision in the dialog that appears

Upon confirmation, the app will immediately:

  • Unregister your device from push notifications
  • Send a deletion request to our servers
  • Clear all locally cached data on your device

By Email

Send a deletion request to developer@syrianhajj.org with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

Data Type Action on Deletion
Account credentials (email, password) Permanently deleted
Personal info (name, phone, National ID, barcode) Permanently deleted
Training progress & certificates Permanently deleted
Complaint history & images Permanently deleted
FCM notification token Unregistered immediately
Local device cache Cleared immediately on-device
After deletion, your data cannot be recovered. Certain data may be retained longer only if required by applicable law.

9. Children's Privacy

Bawabat Al Hajj is intended for use by adults (18+) participating in Hajj pilgrimages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, including countries where Firebase infrastructure is hosted by Google LLC (United States). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where required under GDPR.

11. Cookies

The App does not use cookies. Local storage is used only for app functionality (e.g., login session, cached preferences) and does not include tracking technologies.
